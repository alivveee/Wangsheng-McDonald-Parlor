McDonald Crispy Chicken

McDonald's signature fried chicken, with crispy skin and tender and tasty chicken meat. Available in A la Carte, PaNas 1, PaNas 2, Panas 2 with Fries, PaMer 5, PaMer 7 options

Mora Meat

A large hunk of minced meat packaged inside of a pastry. When you split the pastry in two, minced meat spills out. The taste of meat grease is all but gone, and the baking process has brought out the sweetness of the pastry. Such pastries are usually stamped with the word "Mora," all the better to represent wealth and fortune.

Chicken-Mushroom Skewer

A skewer of mushrooms and poultry. Fresh poultry is complimented by fragrant mushrooms. Don't be picky, chow down!

Golden Chicken Burger

A hamburger in which the fowl meat is the main star. The fluffy bread sandwiches slices of fresh vegetables and a flavorful, crispy fowl fillet. No utensils required here: Just grab it in your hands and bite down on that bundle of rich, satisfying succulence with a hearty "ker-runch"!
